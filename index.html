WEB ACCESSIBILITY SYDNEY

We are Sydney's Web Accessibility community, a friendly and welcoming group of people who are passionate about improving digital products, digital services and web.

Who we are

Our community consists of individuals who have lived experiences with disabilities, accessibility professionals, researchers, advocates, user experience professionals, visual and content designers, developers, and testers.

Web A11Y Sydney is run by volunteers. All our events are free for general puplic. Venues and catering for our events are generously provided by our friends and fellow accessibility supporters.

Our goals are to:

  • Promote awareness and understanding of web accessibility.
  • Create a safe space for networking and making new friends. We have a Code of Conduct to ensure everyone feels comfortable and respected.
  • Share accessibility knowledge through in-person talks and online.
  • Support people who want to give their first public talk on accessibility.

Learn about web accessibility, join our community create great products,
make a difference!
