Welcome to Web A11Y Sydney

We are Sydney's Web Accessibility community, a friendly and welcoming group of people who are passionate about improving digital products, digital services and web. Our community consists of individuals who have lived experiences with disabilities, accessibility professionals, researchers, advocates, user experience professionals, visual and content designers, developers, and testers.

Our goal is to:

  • Promote awareness and understanding of web accessibility.
  • Share accessibility knowledge through in-person talks and online.
  • Create a safe space for networking and making new friends. We have a Code of Conduct to ensure everyone feels comfortable and respected.
  • Support people who want to give their first public talk on accessibility.

Learn about web accessibility, join our community create great products, make a difference!
